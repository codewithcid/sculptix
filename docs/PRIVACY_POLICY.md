# Sculptix — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 3 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Sculptix** ("we", "us", the "App") collects,
uses, and protects your information. By using Sculptix you agree to this policy.

## Who we are

Sculptix is a fitness application that helps you plan workouts, track training,
log nutrition, record body weight, and store progress photos.

- **Contact:** sidhardh512@gmail.com

## Information we collect

We only collect what you provide to use the App:

| Data | Why we collect it |
| ---- | ----------------- |
| **Account** — email address (and password, stored hashed by our auth provider) | To create and secure your account and sign you in. |
| **Profile** — name, age, gender, height, weight, training experience, equipment, goals | To generate your personalised program and nutrition targets. |
| **Fitness activity** — workouts, sets, reps, weights, personal records | To track your training and progress. |
| **Health data you enter** — body weight, body-fat %, nutrition/calorie logs | To show trends and calculate targets. |
| **Progress photos** — images you choose to upload | To let you visually track your physique over time. |

We do **not** collect data in the background. We do **not** access your camera
roll except for photos you explicitly pick to upload. We do **not** use
advertising identifiers.

## How your data is stored and processed

Your data is stored using **Supabase** (our backend and database provider) on
your behalf. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

- **Database access** is restricted by Row Level Security so that you can only
  ever read or modify your own records.
- **Progress photos** are stored in a private storage bucket and are accessible
  only to you via short-lived signed links.

## How we use your data

We use your data solely to provide the App's features (programming, tracking,
analytics, and progress visualisation). We **do not sell** your personal data,
and we **do not share** it with third parties for advertising.

We use no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in this version of the App.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained while your account exists. **You can permanently delete
your account and all associated data at any time:**

- In the app: **Profile → Settings → Delete account.**
- Or by emailing **sidhardh512@gmail.com** with the subject "Delete my account".

Deletion is immediate and irreversible: it removes your profile, programs,
workouts, nutrition logs, weight history, and progress photos.

## Children's privacy

Sculptix is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in
your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption in
transit and strict per-user access controls. No method of transmission or
storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by the "Last updated" date above.
